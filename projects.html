Healthcare Tool Implementation & Compliance Analytics Dashboard Integration

Cigna | 2024
Domain: Healthcare Compliance Budget: $2.1M Duration: 6.5 months Team Size: 14 members Methodology: Agile + SDLC Hybrid

Project Overview

Led a comprehensive project to transform Cigna's compliance reporting and healthcare tool integration. The existing processes were slow, manual, and error-prone, increasing audit risks and operational inefficiencies. Delivered a suite of four critical healthcare tools and interactive Power BI dashboards providing real-time visibility into compliance metrics, audit readiness, and staff training completion.

Business Challenge

Manual compliance reporting was creating significant audit risks, while disconnected tools led to inconsistent data and poor visibility. The organization faced 30% higher effort due to rework from misaligned tools and user needs. With increasing regulatory scrutiny (HIPAA audits, CMS requirements) and industry-wide push toward automation and analytics in healthcare compliance, Cigna needed faster, accurate compliance reporting and unified tools for seamless data sharing.

UberEats Student Advantage – Product Strategy & Implementation Framework

Academic Project | Product Strategy
Role: Project Manager / Product Strategy Lead Focus: Student-Centric Product Enhancement Approach: End-to-End Concept to Execution

Project Overview

Designed and validated a student-centric feature enhancement for UberEats aimed at increasing adoption, retention, and order frequency among university students. Created a comprehensive "Student Advantage" offering combining exclusive discounts, flexible meal plans, referral incentives, and local restaurant partnerships. The project covered end-to-end thinking: market research, visual communication, stakeholder alignment, phased implementation planning, and structured feedback loops.

Problem Statement

Student Pain Points

  • Cost sensitivity and inconsistent discounts across platforms
  • Lack of predictable meal planning options
  • Low perceived differentiation between food delivery platforms

Business Challenges

  • High churn rate in the student segment
  • Low lifetime value compared to working professionals
  • Weak campus-level brand stickiness and loyalty

Solution: UberEats Student Advantage

Conceptualized a targeted program with four core pillars designed to address both user needs and business objectives:

1. Exclusive Student Offers

  • Campus-verified student discounts
  • Limited-time promotional codes
  • Strategic partnerships with local restaurants near universities

2. Flexible Meal Plans

  • Prepaid and subscription-based meal bundles
  • Customizable plans based on student schedules
  • Predictable monthly spending for budget management

3. Referral & Group Incentives

  • Refer-a-friend rewards program
  • Group discounts for shared orders
  • Campus Ambassador Program for organic growth

4. Visual & Content-Driven Experience

  • Infographics highlighting value propositions
  • End-to-end storyboard showing student journey
  • Clear visual hierarchy for quick benefit communication

My Responsibilities & Strategic Approach

Product & Strategy:

  • Defined value proposition and success metrics for student-focused feature enhancement
  • Translated user pain points into concrete product capabilities and features
  • Structured offerings to balance user value delivery with business sustainability

Project Planning & Execution:

  • Built comprehensive 4-phase implementation roadmap (Planning & Market Research → Design & Development → Pilot Testing → Full Rollout)
  • Created detailed implementation steps including project team formation, timeline and milestones, resource allocation, training and operational support, monitoring and evaluation frameworks

Stakeholder Engagement:

  • Identified and mapped key stakeholders (university partners, restaurant partners, internal product/marketing/design teams)
  • Designed engagement strategies ensuring alignment on goals, continuous feedback loops, and win-win outcomes for all parties

Feedback & Iteration:

  • Designed multi-channel feedback mechanisms (student surveys, focus groups, A/B testing, stakeholder interviews)
  • Used feedback to refine visuals, improve feature usability, and adjust rollout strategies
